MAKARA SANKRANTHI

Festival of telugu people

Sankranthi is a festival celebrated all over over India with different names at the same time. For Telugu people it is a really important festival celebrated for three days when the direction of sun changes from dakshnayan to uttarayan and the sun enters makara rasi.

It is celebrated with different attributes ranging from rangavellis to flying kites etc. People also prepare eddibiles in a big quantity for celebrating the festival.
